## Hypothesis

An adversary is orchestrating a coordinated campaign of investor, media, and analyst commentary to pressure the organisation into making security-adverse decisions. Evidence will appear as unusual clustering of narrative themes from newly-created accounts, coordinated timing of critical commentary across multiple platforms, and pressure specifically targeting security investment or security leadership.

---

## Hunt Queries

### Hunt 1 — Newly-Created Analyst/Media Accounts Publishing Security-Critical Commentary

```sql
SELECT account_handle, platform, account_creation_date,
       article_publication_date, article_title, article_sentiment,
       DATEDIFF(article_publication_date, account_creation_date) as days_since_creation
FROM media_monitoring_log
WHERE article_topic ILIKE '%security%'
   OR article_topic ILIKE '%CISO%'
   OR article_topic ILIKE '%cybersecurity investment%'
AND days_since_creation < 180  -- Account < 6 months old
AND article_sentiment = 'negative'
AND organisation_mentioned = true
AND article_publication_date >= NOW() - INTERVAL 90 DAYS
ORDER BY days_since_creation ASC
```

### Hunt 2 — Coordinated Timing of Critical Commentary Across Platforms

```sql
SELECT COUNT(DISTINCT platform) as platform_count,
       array_agg(DISTINCT platform) as platforms,
       COUNT(*) as article_count,
       MIN(publication_timestamp) as first_publication,
       MAX(publication_timestamp) as last_publication
FROM media_monitoring_log
WHERE article_sentiment = 'negative'
AND article_topic ILIKE '%security%'
AND organisation_mentioned = true
AND publication_timestamp >= NOW() - INTERVAL 90 DAYS
GROUP BY DATE_TRUNC('week', publication_timestamp)
HAVING platform_count >= 3 AND article_count >= 5
ORDER BY article_count DESC
```

### Hunt 3 — Board/Investor Pressure on Security Budget Correlated with External Commentary

```sql
SELECT b.meeting_date, b.agenda_item, b.outcome,
       COUNT(m.article_id) as negative_articles_prior_week
FROM board_meeting_records b
LEFT JOIN media_monitoring_log m ON
  m.publication_timestamp BETWEEN b.meeting_date - INTERVAL 7 DAYS AND b.meeting_date
  AND m.article_sentiment = 'negative'
  AND m.article_topic ILIKE '%security%'
WHERE b.agenda_item ILIKE '%security%'
AND b.meeting_date >= NOW() - INTERVAL 12 MONTHS
ORDER BY negative_articles_prior_week DESC
```
