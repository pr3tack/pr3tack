# Hunt: Procurement Account Establishment — Fraudulent Vendor Registration Signals

## Metadata

| Field | Value |

---

## Hypothesis

An adversary has registered a fraudulent vendor identity in the target's procurement or vendor management system — gaining trusted vendor status to access vendor portals, submit fraudulent invoices, or establish a pretext for social engineering.

---

## Hunt Queries

### Hunt 1 — New Vendor with Name Similar to Existing Approved Vendor

```sql
SELECT nv.vendor_name, nv.registration_date, nv.contact_email, nv.bank_account,
       ev.vendor_name as similar_to, levenshtein(nv.vendor_name, ev.vendor_name) as edit_distance
FROM vendor_registry nv
CROSS JOIN vendor_registry ev
WHERE nv.registration_date >= NOW() - INTERVAL 90 DAYS
AND levenshtein(nv.vendor_name, ev.vendor_name) BETWEEN 1 AND 3
AND nv.vendor_id != ev.vendor_id
AND ev.registration_date < NOW() - INTERVAL 365 DAYS  -- established vendors only
ORDER BY edit_distance ASC, nv.registration_date DESC
```

### Hunt 2 — New Vendor Invoice Within 30 Days of Registration

```sql
SELECT v.vendor_name, v.registration_date, i.invoice_date, i.invoice_amount,
       i.invoice_number, DATEDIFF(i.invoice_date, v.registration_date) as days_to_first_invoice
FROM vendor_registry v
JOIN invoices i ON v.vendor_id = i.vendor_id
WHERE v.registration_date >= NOW() - INTERVAL 90 DAYS
AND DATEDIFF(i.invoice_date, v.registration_date) < 30
ORDER BY i.invoice_amount DESC
```

### Hunt 3 — Vendor Portal Access to Out-of-Scope Document Types

```sql
SELECT vendor_id, vendor_name, document_type, access_timestamp
FROM vendor_portal_access_log
WHERE document_type IN ('internal_rfp', 'procurement_calendar',
                        'internal_contact_directory', 'budget_forecast')
AND vendor_registration_date >= NOW() - INTERVAL 180 DAYS
ORDER BY access_timestamp DESC
```

### Hunt 4 — Bank Account Change on Established Vendor

```sql
SELECT vendor_name, vendor_id, change_date, old_bank_account,
       new_bank_account, changed_by_user, change_request_source
FROM vendor_bank_account_changes
WHERE change_date >= NOW() - INTERVAL 90 DAYS
ORDER BY change_date DESC
```

## Expected Findings

| Finding | Priority |
|---|---|
| New vendor name within edit-distance 2 of established vendor | Critical |
| Invoice within 30 days of registration for > $10k | High |
| New vendor accessing internal procurement documents | High |
| Bank account change on established vendor | Critical |

---

## Tuning Notes

- Hunt 4 (bank account changes) is the highest-fidelity signal — legitimate vendors rarely change bank accounts and always do so through documented processes; any change warrants human verification via a confirmed phone call to a known contact
- Establish a minimum 30-day no-invoice window for all new vendor registrations as a preventive control
