# Detection: Anticipatory Brand/Domain Squatting — Look-Alike Domain Registration

## Metadata

| Field | Value |
|---|---|

| **Tactic** | PT14 — Pre-Positioning |

| **Data Source** | Certificate Transparency Logs / Domain Registration Monitoring / Email Gateway |
| **Confidence** | High |
| **Status** | Draft |

---

## Objective

Detect registration of look-alike domains targeting the organisation by monitoring certificate transparency logs and domain registration feeds for domains with high visual similarity to owned brand assets.

---

## Detection Logic

```yaml
title: Look-Alike Domain Detected in Certificate Transparency Logs
description: >
  Detects registration of a domain with high visual similarity (edit distance
  ≤ 2, homoglyph substitution, or combosquat pattern) to an owned brand domain,
  identified via certificate transparency log monitoring. Requires integration
  with a CT log monitoring service (crt.sh, Facebook CT Monitor, or commercial
  domain monitoring).
logsource:
  category: external_feed
  product: ct_log_monitor
detection:
  selection:
    event_type: 'new_certificate'
    similarity_score|gte: 0.85   # Fuzzy similarity to owned domains
    is_owned_domain: false
  condition: selection
level: high
tags:
  - pr3tack.PT14
  - pr3tack.PT14.002
---
title: Inbound Email from Look-Alike Domain
description: >
  Detects inbound email where the sending domain has high visual similarity
  to an owned domain — indicative of an activated squatted domain being used
  for phishing or BEC.
logsource:
  category: email
  product: email_gateway
detection:
  selection:
    sender_domain_similarity_score|gte: 0.85
    sender_domain_is_owned: false
    dmarc_result: 'none'
  condition: selection
level: critical
tags:
  - pr3tack.PT14
  - pr3tack.PT14.002
```

---

## Tuning Notes

- CT log monitoring requires integration with crt.sh, Facebook CT Monitor, or a commercial service (DomainTools, CSC)
- The similarity threshold (0.85) should be calibrated against your domain portfolio — very short brand names may require a lower threshold
- Email gateway integration requires the gateway to perform domain similarity scoring at ingest time (available in Proofpoint, Mimecast, and Microsoft Defender for O365)

---

## Validation

1. Register a test typosquat domain (e.g., add a character to your brand domain in a non-critical TLD)
2. Issue a certificate for the test domain and confirm the CT log monitor detects it
3. Send a test email from the look-alike domain and confirm the email gateway rule fires
