# Detection: Holiday/Travel Window Triggering — Off-Hours Privileged Access

## Metadata

| Field | Value |
|---|---|
| **Technique** | PT18.001 — Holiday/Travel Window Triggering |
| **Tactic** | PT18 — Operational Tempo Manipulation |
| **Detection Type** | Rule |
| **Data Source** | IAM Audit Logs / EDR / SIEM |
| **Confidence** | High |
| **Status** | Draft |

---

## Objective

Detect attack operations timed to coincide with holiday and reduced-staffing periods by alerting on privileged access and high-risk operations during known holiday windows and after-hours periods.

---

## Detection Logic

```yaml
title: Privileged Account Access During Holiday Period
description: >
  Detects use of privileged accounts or administrative access during
  configured holiday periods or public holidays where SOC coverage is
  reduced. Adversaries deliberately time operations to coincide with
  staffing gaps to extend their exploitation window.
logsource:
  category: authentication
detection:
  selection:
    event_type:
      - 'privileged_login'
      - 'admin_console_access'
      - 'break_glass_account_use'
    is_holiday_period: true  # Populated via SIEM calendar enrichment
  filter_oncall:
    user_id|in:
      - 'oncall-rota-list'  # Populated from on-call management system
  condition: selection and not filter_oncall
level: high
tags:
  - pr3tack.PT18
  - pr3tack.PT18.001
---
title: High-Risk Operations Outside Business Hours on Eve of Holiday
description: >
  Detects high-risk operations (mass data access, new persistence, privilege
  escalation) occurring in the 6-hour window before a major public holiday
  or long weekend — a common adversarial pre-positioning pattern.
logsource:
  category: cloud
detection:
  selection:
    event_type:
      - 'iam_create_user'
      - 'iam_attach_admin_policy'
      - 'scheduled_task_created'
      - 'new_service_installed'
    hours_until_holiday|lte: 6
  condition: selection
level: high
tags:
  - pr3tack.PT18
  - pr3tack.PT18.001
```

---

## Tuning Notes

- Integrate with your HR/calendar system to populate the `is_holiday_period` enrichment field with your organisation's holiday calendar
- Integrate with your on-call management system (PagerDuty, OpsGenie) to populate the on-call filter
- Increase alert sensitivity thresholds across all rules during holiday periods — lower the bar for investigation

---

## Validation

1. During a test period, simulate a break-glass account login and confirm the holiday period rule fires
2. Create a test scheduled task in the 6-hour pre-holiday window and confirm the pre-positioning rule fires
