# PT02.001 — AI-Generated Persona Synthesis

## Metadata

| Field | Value |
|---|---|
| **ID** | PT02.001 |
| **Tactic** | PT02 — Resource Development |
| **Sub-techniques** | Synthetic Identity Creation, AI Headshot Generation |
| **MITRE Gap** | MITRE covers phishing and identity techniques but does not address the use of Generative AI to industrially synthesise high-fidelity fake identities that bypass both manual and automated "New User" scrutiny. |
| **Response Priority** | Medium |
| **Status** | Draft |

---

## Description

Using Generative AI tools to create high-fidelity, non-existent identities — including photorealistic headshots, fabricated LinkedIn employment histories, and consistent cross-platform personas — to bypass "New User" scrutiny by HR, Security, and vendor-onboarding processes. These synthetic identities are then used as the basis for spear-phishing campaigns, social engineering operations, or fraudulent contractor/vendor relationships.

---

## Adversary Value

- Bypasses manual identity verification that relies on profile photo authenticity and social media presence
- AI-generated headshots have no reverse-image-search matches, defeating a common detection method
- Fabricated employment histories can be constructed to be contextually plausible for the target industry
- Dramatically reduces the resource cost of creating convincing false identities at scale
- Enables long-term persistent access via fraudulent contractor or vendor relationships

---

## Why It's Not in MITRE ATT&CK

MITRE's Establish Accounts technique covers the creation of accounts for adversary use but does not capture the use of **Generative AI** to create high-fidelity synthetic trust. The automation of identity synthesis — combining AI imagery, LLM-generated histories, and platform manipulation — represents a qualitatively new capability beyond MITRE's existing "Phishing" or "Identity" categories.

---

## Procedure Examples

1. Generate a photorealistic headshot using a GAN-based or diffusion-model image generator (e.g., This Person Does Not Exist)
2. Construct a plausible employment history using an LLM, tailored to the target industry and role
3. Create consistent accounts across LinkedIn, GitHub, X, and relevant professional communities
4. Age the persona for 30–90 days with low-volume, authentic-seeming activity before use
5. Initiate contact with the target organisation via the synthetic identity for contractor, vendor, or collaboration purposes

---

## Countermeasures

### Prevention

| Control | Description |
|---|---|
| **Enhanced Identity Verification** | Require video-call verification or government-ID-backed identity proofing for any new vendor contact, contractor, or external collaborator requesting access to internal systems. |
| **Reverse Image Search Policies** | Train HR and Security teams to perform reverse image searches (Google, TinEye, AI-generated face detectors) on profile photos of unknown contacts before granting access or engaging in business relationships. |
| **AI-Detection Tooling** | Deploy AI-generated content detection tools on inbound communications and profile submissions (headshot analysis, writing-style anomaly detection). |

### Detection

| Control | Description |
|---|---|
| **Profile Anomaly Scoring** | Flag new contacts whose LinkedIn profile creation date, connection count, endorsement patterns, or employment history exhibit statistical anomalies consistent with synthetic personas. |
| **Cross-Platform Consistency Checks** | Correlate claimed identities across multiple platforms — inconsistencies in profile photos, employment dates, or activity patterns are strong indicators. |

### Response

- Quarantine all access and communications associated with a confirmed synthetic persona
- Notify downstream teams and partners who may have interacted with the persona
